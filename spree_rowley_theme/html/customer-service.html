Return Policy

We will accept returns without question within 30 days. Please contact Customer Service at 877.979.9200 or customerservice@rowleyeyewear.com and they will be happy to assist you with next steps.

If you would like to return your order after the first 30 days, please contact Customer Service to discuss your return. Once your return or exchange request has been accepted, we will email you a prepaid return shipping label. We ask that you print and attach it to your return package.
