Are frames available in different sizes?
Each style is available in one size which is indicated in the product description. However, the collection offers a large variety of sizes – try-on up to four at a time with our Try it On option.
Can you explain the different measurements listed in the product description?
The measurements in the product description correspond in the following order: width of lens– width of bridge – length of the temples. Each is measured in millimeters.
Are frames available without prescription lenses?
Of course! In fact, we encourage you to experiment with different styles – that is one of the reasons we are so excited to offer Rowley Care. To order your frames without prescription lenses, just select the desired option during the checkout process.
Can I get frames with bifocal or progressive lenses?
Yes, just select the desired option when entering your prescription during the checkout process.
What about Transitions lenses?
Yes, as a fashion brand, we want to make sure you have as many options as possible when building your eyewear wardrobe.
Are frames available with hi-index lenses?
Yes, if your prescription falls outside of the +4/-4 range, we will automatically default you to hi-index lenses. You can also manually select hi-index lenses during the checkout process.
And can I get polarized lenses?
Yes! You can select this option during the checkout process.
What if my lenses get dirty?
Each Rowley Eyewear frame comes with a micro-fiber cleaning cloth which can be used to clean smudges, dust, etc. You can also opt to clean your lenses by rinsing them with temperate water and soap. It is important to avoid using hot water as they can damage your lenses.
What should I do about a scratched lens?
Lenses obviously get scratched with normal use. We will happily replace your lenses free of charge if they get scratched within the first six months of your purchase. After that time period, if you are a member of Rowley Care, you can replace your frames for half the price of a new pair. Please contact Customer Service at 877.979.9200 or customerservice@rowleyeyewear.com for more information.

What are my options for providing my prescription?
You have a few options to choose from for this portion of the ordering process. You can either:
  1. Enter your prescription in the fields provided during checkout.

Can you explain “pupillary distance”?
Pupillary distance (PD) is the distance between the centers of each pupil in millimeters.

This measurement is used in your prescription to ensure that you don’t have difficulty focusing. If you already have your PD measurement, please enter it during checkout. If you do not have this measurement, Rowley Eyewear will utilize industry standard defaults and fill your prescription accordingly.

If you would like additional information, please contact Customer Service at 877.979.9200 or customerservice@rowleyeyewear.com and we will be happy to assist you further.
Will my order be verified to ensure everything is correct?
Of course! Our website utilizes a complex set of rules in order to ensure that your prescription is entered flawlessly. If certain measurements don’t seem correct, you will be prompted to double check those numbers. We also have a dedicated Customer Service team who is checking everything on our end as well. This is best done if we have a copy (scan, photo, etc) of your actual prescription.

Finally, if you live in a state that requires prescription verification (AK, AL, AZ, CA, CT, FL, GA, KY, MA, NC, NJ, NV, NY, OH, RI, SC, TN, VT, WA) we will also contact your doctor to confirm your prescription.
How does it work?
Some people like to try on everything before they buy – and eyewear is no different. The Try Them On option allows you to preview up to four frames in your home before placing an order. Frames are sent to you with demo lenses so that you can decide which style (or styles!) best suit you.

Please note that you’ll be asked for a credit card during check-out. It is used for identity verification and will only be charged in the event that the sample frames are not returned. IMPORTANT: Debit cards can NOT be used for our Try Them On program.

Once you receive your frames, you will have seven days to try-on the frames you selected.

At the end of your Try Them On period, simply mail back the package with the frames and sign back in to your account to make your selections and input prescription information. Please note that your credit card will be billed if the sample frames are not returned.
How long do I have to Try Them On?
Our Try Them On program allows you to keep the frames for up to seven business days. We ask that you send them back at the conclusion of that time period.
Why do you ask for my credit card information if Try Them On is free?
The Try Them On option is completely free but we do need your credit card information to verify your identity. This card will only be charged in the event that the sample frames are not returned after the seven day period.
Will any charges appear on my credit card?
There will be a $1 hold authorization. Once you return the frames, it will be taken off your card. We do, however, reserve the right to charge you for any unreturned samples at the conclusion of the Try Them On period. Please contact our Customer Service department at 877.979.9200 or customerservice@rowleyeyewear.com for any additional information.
If I like a frame that I ordered for Try Them On, can I keep this and send back the ones I didn’t like?
Please send back all frames that you received for Try Them On as they are only samples. Once you’ve made your selection, please place an order on our website and we will send you a brand new pair with your selected prescription information (if any), packaging, etc. Shipping is always free!
